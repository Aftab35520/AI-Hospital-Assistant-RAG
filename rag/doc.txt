# Sunrise Multispeciality Hospital

## Overview

Sunrise Multispeciality Hospital is a modern tertiary-care healthcare institution located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 2012, the hospital was founded with the vision of providing affordable, patient-centered, and technology-driven healthcare services. Over the years, Sunrise Multispeciality Hospital has become one of the region's trusted healthcare providers by combining experienced medical professionals, advanced diagnostic facilities, and compassionate patient care.

The hospital operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing emergency care, outpatient consultations, inpatient services, diagnostic testing, surgical procedures, rehabilitation, preventive healthcare, and telemedicine consultations. It serves patients from Lucknow and neighboring districts, including Barabanki, Sitapur, Unnao, Rae Bareli, Hardoi, and Kanpur.

The hospital is committed to delivering high-quality healthcare while maintaining transparency, ethical medical practices, patient safety, and continuous innovation.

# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book an appointment online?**

Yes. Appointments can be booked through the AI assistant, hospital reception, or telephone.

**Q: Is emergency service available at night?**

Yes. Emergency services operate 24×7.

**Q: Do I need a referral?**

No. Patients may directly book appointments with specialists unless a specific department requires a referral.

**Q: What should I bring for my first visit?**

Bring your patient ID (if available), identification document, previous medical records, prescriptions, and insurance information.

**Q: Can I cancel my appointment?**

Yes. Appointments may be cancelled before the scheduled consultation, subject to hospital policy.

**Q: Does the hospital provide diagnostic tests?**

Yes. The hospital offers pathology, radiology, cardiac diagnostics, and preventive health screening services.
